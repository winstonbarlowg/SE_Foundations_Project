Statistics by Gender

A data visualisation project to raise awareness about human trafficking worldwide

Thanks to the Counter Trafficking
The purpose of this webiste is to understand the impact of trafficking by visualising key data points.

More than half of all the female cases globally are sexually exploited, with the primary cause being lured into a false relationship.

Male trafficked victims are predominantly exploited under forced labour such as in agriculture or manufacturing.

At any given time in 2016, an estimated 40.3 million people are in modern slavery, including 24.9 million in forced labour and 15.4 million in forced marriage.
